\# 📜 Deheritance Litepaper



\## A New Way to Protect, Preserve, and Pass On What Matters



---



\## 1. Introduction



Every human being leaves something behind.



Not just money or property, but:



\* Memories

\* Messages

\* Documents

\* Knowledge

\* Wishes

\* Values

\* Stories

\* Responsibilities



Yet in the digital age, much of what matters most is fragile, scattered, or lost forever.



\*\*Deheritance exists to solve one simple, universal problem:\*\*



> \*How do we make sure the most important parts of our lives reach the people they are meant for, at the right time, even when we are no longer here?\*



---



\## 2. The Problem



Today, people store their lives across:



\* Phones

\* Computers

\* Cloud accounts

\* Email

\* Social platforms

\* Hard drives

\* Paper documents



When something unexpected happens:



\* Families don’t know where things are

\* Passwords are lost

\* Important documents are inaccessible

\* Memories disappear

\* Wishes are misunderstood or never found



Even when there is a will, \*\*the emotional and digital side of a life is often missing.\*\*



We are creating more data than any generation before us, yet we have \*\*no reliable way to pass it on.\*\*



---



\## 3. The Vision



Deheritance envisions a world where:



\* Everyone can prepare a \*\*digital legacy\*\*

\* Important information is \*\*not lost\*\*

\* Personal wishes are \*\*respected\*\*

\* Families are \*\*protected from chaos and uncertainty\*\*

\* Memories are \*\*preserved across generations\*\*

\* No single company, institution, or authority can decide what survives and what disappears



Deheritance is built around one core belief:



> \*Your life story, your wishes, and your knowledge should not depend on any single organization or system to survive.\*



---



\## 4. What is Deheritance?



Deheritance is a \*\*digital inheritance system\*\* that allows people to:



\* Securely prepare important information

\* Decide \*\*who should receive it\*\*

\* Decide \*\*when it should be accessible\*\*

\* Ensure it is \*\*not altered, lost, or hidden\*\*

\* Make sure it is \*\*released exactly as intended\*\*



Unlike traditional services:



\* Deheritance does \*\*not rely on one company\*\*

\* There is \*\*no central place where everything can disappear\*\*

\* There is \*\*no third party that must be trusted to keep your legacy alive\*\*

\* The system is designed so that \*\*no single actor can control, block, or rewrite your legacy\*\*



---



\## 5. How It Works (In Human Terms)



1\. You gather what matters:



&nbsp;  \* Documents

&nbsp;  \* Messages

&nbsp;  \* Instructions

&nbsp;  \* Memories

&nbsp;  \* Knowledge



2\. You decide:



&nbsp;  \* Who should receive what

&nbsp;  \* Under what conditions

&nbsp;  \* At what time or moment



3\. You place them into your personal Deheritance vault.



4\. While you are alive:



&nbsp;  \* Everything stays private

&nbsp;  \* Only you control it



5\. When the chosen moment arrives:



&nbsp;  \* Your information is released

&nbsp;  \* Exactly as you intended

&nbsp;  \* To the people you chose



No bureaucracy.

No dependence on a single institution.

No need to trust a company to “still exist.”
