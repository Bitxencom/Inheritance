# Inheritance Services

## What is a Vault?

A vault is a will document that is created, stored, or managed in electronic form. The content is the same as a conventional will, which is a person's statement about the distribution of assets or special messages that take effect after they pass away.

## What can be included in an Inheritance?

### Traditional Financial Assets
- Savings & Bank Accounts
- Deposits
- Stocks & Bonds
- Mutual Funds / Unit Links
- Insurance (life/inheritance claim benefits)

### Digital Financial Assets
- E-wallets: PayPal, Venmo, Cash App, etc.
- Cryptocurrency: Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.

### Digital & Online Assets
- Domains & Websites
- Social Media Accounts: Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter), TikTok, etc.
- Email Accounts
- Cloud Storage: Google Drive, iCloud, Dropbox, etc.
- Game Accounts & Virtual Property: Steam, Epic Games, Mobile Legends, Free Fire, etc.
- NFT & Digital Collections

### Intellectual Property Rights
- Copyright: books, music, software, designs
- Patents / Trademarks
- Digital Royalties: YouTube, Spotify, Play Store, App Store, etc.

### Special Instructions & Messages
- Messages to family (moral values, life lessons, prayers)
- Funeral instructions (location, procedures, specific customs)
- End-of-life medical instructions (e.g., refusal of certain medical equipment)
- Charity & endowment instructions (percentage of assets for donations)
- Child guardian designation (if there are minor children)

## How to Create a Vault

1. **Vault Details**
   - Choose vault type: Permanent (One-time) or Editable
   - Enter vault title/name
   - Write vault content

2. **Security Questions**
   - Create 3-5 security questions with their answers
   - Questions and answers are created by you
   - Used for verification when opening the inheritance

3. **Opening Time (Release Trigger)**
   - **Specific date**: Choose a future date (5/10/15/20 years or custom)
   - **Anytime**: Manual release anytime with the correct keys

4. **Payment**
   - Choose payment method: **Wander Wallet**

5. **Save Important Information**
   - **Vault ID**: Unique identifier for your inheritance
   - **5 Fraction Keys**: Encryption keys that must be distributed to trusted individuals
   - Copy each fraction key and manually distribute to at least 3 different people

## Data Security

Your inheritance data is protected with **multi-layer encryption**:

- **Post-Quantum Cryptography (ML-KEM-768)**: Future-proof protection against quantum computer attacks
- **AES-256 Encryption**: Military-grade encryption standard
- **Shamir Secret Sharing**: Keys split into 5 parts with 3-of-5 threshold
- **Blockchain Storage**: Permanent, immutable storage

Only with the correct combination of Vault ID, security questions, and at least 3 fraction keys can the vault be opened.
